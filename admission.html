Admission Requirements

Students willing to register at Uni should fulfill the following requirements:

Education

Must have Completed Secondary School Certificate accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education.

Certificate

Should bring the original Secondary School Leaving Certificate.

Include two (2) photocopies of the certificate.

Photos

Should bring four (4) passport-size photos.

Background must be white.

Registration Fees

Medicine & General Surgery = $35

Computer & IT = $30

Other Faculties = $25

Note: Fees are non-refundable.

Experience

For part-time program applicants, 2 years of work experience is recommended.

Strong time management skills and employer support may be required.
